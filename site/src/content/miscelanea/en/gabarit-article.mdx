## Section

This file is a **template**: copy it to start an article, then remove
`draft: true` (and declare the `provenance:`) to publish. It also keeps the
collection non-empty while no article is live (the content loader warns at
build time otherwise).

<Note title="Reminder">
  Every French article has its English translation under `en/` (project rule).
</Note>
